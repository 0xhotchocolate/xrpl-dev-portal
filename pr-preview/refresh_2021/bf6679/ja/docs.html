Hot Topic: NFTs

Interested in non-fungible tokens, but concerned about their large carbon footprint? Check out these proposed standards for issuing NFTs on the XRP Ledger:

  • XLS-14d: Non fungible tokens (indivisible NFT's) on the XRPL
  • XLS-19d: Wallet based Proof of Digital Asset Property and Rights (NFT)
  • XLS-20d: Non-Fungible Token Support
